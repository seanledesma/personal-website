Reflection

I hope you’ve enjoyed taking a look at my website. This site represents a lot of my time and work, and it’s something I’ve come to be proud of. The links to my past projects are in chronological order starting at the top, so you can see how my work has progressed. I’ve gone from knowing nothing of web development to becoming a fairly decent beginner. It is really amazing to start from basically nothing and create a website that I can show to my family and friends. It’s frustrating, difficult, time-consuming, but very rewarding. The feeling of finally discovering the solution to a problem is amazing. I am very glad I took this class, and am looking forward to the next one. -Sean Ledesma
